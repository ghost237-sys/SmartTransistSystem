The problem we're solving

Standing at the stage,
not knowing if there's room.

Every day, millions of Kenyans wait at bus stages without knowing if the next bus has a seat, when it will arrive, or whether they've already been overcharged. SmartTransit fixes all three.

How it works

Three steps to a confirmed seat.

From your phone to your seat in under two minutes.

1

Search your route

Enter where you're going and when. See available buses, seat counts, departure times, and live prices — all in one place.

2

Pay with M-Pesa

Choose your seat, enter your phone number, and confirm your M-Pesa PIN. Your seat is locked the moment payment clears.

3

Track and board

Watch your bus on a live map. When it arrives, show your QR code to the conductor and board — your seat is already waiting.
